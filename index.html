- - - MY PET IS MY CLOSEST COMPANION - - -

To advocate and offer the resources, knowledge, and programs necessary to end the slaughter of companion animals and to create a culture where animals are valued and treated with respect and kindness.

To build a society where unwanted and abused animals are given a voice and a second opportunity at a happy and permanent home, and where anybody who can help will do so.
